VOTING INFORMATION

    To register to vote in Oregon, you must be:
  • At least 16 years old. You cannot vote until you are 18 years old.
  • A citizen of the United States as of at least 21 days prior to the voter registration deadline.
  • An Oregon resident.

From: Oregon DMV

    How to vote:
  • Locate your polling place and make sure you've registered to vote.
  • Enter polling place.
  • Ask for ballot or location of machine.
NOTE: For Absentee and Early Voting, see your state website.

From: How To Vote
